About Me

I'm an American-Dutch developer, with a passion for solving real world problems using effective, creative, efficient, scalable solutions. An avid fan of Lego's when I was a child, these inspired and encouraged me to apply my urge to create in a more technical manner. Around my 11th birthday I began saving for parts and completed my first desktop computer build at 12 years old. Hooked on the creative opportunities afforded by this new found technological realm, I developed a wide array of technical interests including, but not limited to networking, machine learning, cryptography, and hardware overclocking.

Born in New York City, I spent my childhood in Manhattan, Iowa City, Santa Barbara, and Amsterdam. After graduating from Dos Pueblos High School in Santa Barbara, I enrolled in The University of Iowa's undergraduate Computer Science program. I am currently working on finishing my BS in Computer Science. While the line between my work and play is often blurred, in my free time I enjoy running, hiking, swimming, rock climbing, reading, board and video games. Currently, my languages of choice for development are Python, Java, C#, and C++.

Enjoy your time here and feel free to contact me for business inquiries, or even just to say hi, using any other method you'd prefer.
